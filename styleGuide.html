PERSONAL BRAND

My audience are professional designers or HR managers familiar with the word of design. They are looking for someone who is sharp, familiar with design patterns, and able to work with a team.

When they visit my site, they will need to know who I am and what my ethos is. They will want to know my skills. They will want to know what kind of work that I have done in the past. And they will need to know how to contact me. This will be done by guiding them through these stages as they browse my site.

My brand will be defined by promoting the concept that I am skilled as a front-facing designer, as well as being skilled in the word of back-end code. I will be the person to bridge these two fields, and I will do so with a playful and artistic flair.

STYLE GUIDE

My website will be one page with a single scroll. It will begin by detailing who I am, move through my work history, and then show how to contact me. The user will be guided through this process one by one.
